I Make Games

Designing and making games offers some of the most difficult and rewarding challenges I have ever faced. Game problems are unique, technical, subjective, and wonderfully complex.

Part art, part engineering, developing games requires someone with a mind for both.

I make video games with Unity and web technologies. I make board games with pen and paper.

A Druid's Duel

Indie game released on Steam, 2015

Designed and built over 15 months, this was my first game and I went all-in. Built in Unity before 2D was really a thing, using the Orthello sprite engine. I learned a tremendous amount about finishing a game, testing, polishing, and the reality of selling a game.

A ton more info available at the website.

Raiders of the Twisted Temple

Prototype 3D Endless Runner

This began as a simple sketch and concept for an endless runner that would include Tetris-like rotatable portions of the path that would serve as challenging obstacles.

I focused on making all of my own assets in Blender including a low-poly character rigged for running and idle animations.
